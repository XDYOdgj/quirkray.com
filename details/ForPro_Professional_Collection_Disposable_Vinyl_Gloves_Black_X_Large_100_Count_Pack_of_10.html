ForPro Professional Collection Disposable Vinyl Gloves

Engineered for heavy-duty use, these industrial-grade disposable vinyl gloves offer top-tier protection and comfort. Perfect for food handlers, healthcare professionals, and workers in demanding environments, they are designed to deliver reliable performance without compromising on safety or user experience.

Built for Tough Conditions

Constructed with thick 2.75 mil palm and 3.9 mil finger material, these gloves are durable enough to handle industrial and commercial tasks. Whether you're cleaning, assembling, or working in a high-risk area, the reinforced construction ensures long-lasting use and protection against oils, chemicals, and other hazardous substances.

Safe and Comfortable Fit

With a powder-free and latex-free formula, these gloves provide a seamless fit for users with sensitive skin, reducing the risk of allergic reactions. The ambidextrous design allows for flexible use in either hand, while the black color and textured surface enhance grip and visibility, even in wet or slippery conditions.

Ideal for Food Handling

These gloves are food-safe, making them an excellent choice for kitchen environments and food service professionals. The non-sterile design is perfect for single-use scenarios, ensuring hygiene and convenience for daily operations.

Convenient Packaging

Each pack includes 100 gloves in a dispenser box, offering easy access and storage for high-volume use. Whether you're running a restaurant, managing a cleaning operation, or working in a factory, these gloves are designed to keep up with your workflow and provide consistent performance.

  • Latex-free and powder-free for sensitive skin
